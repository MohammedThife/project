Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Arun Teja

BDS  ·  MDS (Endodontics)  ·  Root Canal Specialist  ·  Smile Design Expert

Dr. Arun Teja completed his BDS from a premier dental institution and subsequently pursued his Masters in Dental Surgery (Endodontics), specializing in Root Canal Therapy.

With over a decade of clinical practice, Dr. Arun has performed thousands of successful procedures, ranging from complex root canal treatments to full-smile makeovers. His expertise in Smile Design has helped hundreds of patients regain their confidence with naturally beautiful smiles.

At Sky Dental, Dr. Arun combines international dental techniques with compassionate, patient-centred care — ensuring every patient feels heard, respected, and well-treated.

Our Clinic

About Sky Dental

A modern, fully-equipped dental clinic designed for your comfort.

Sky Dental was founded with a single vision — to make high-quality dental care accessible, comfortable, and affordable for everyone in the Nallagandla–Gachibowil area.

The clinic is equipped with digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, state-of-the-art sterilization equipment, and modern dental chairs — all in a soothing, welcoming environment.

We serve patients across Gachibowil, Nallagandla, Tellapur, Kokapet, and the broader Hyderabad western suburbs.
